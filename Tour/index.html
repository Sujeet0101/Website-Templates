What's Our Story?

Based in New Baneshwor, Kathmandu, Oncall Travel and Tours may be a young name in Nepal's travel industry, but in just 2-3 years, we have established ourselves as a provider of world-class travel services. Our focus has been on delivering exceptional vehicle rental services to tourists, allowing them to explore the beauty of Nepal with ease and comfort.

Whether you're seeking the freedom of independent travel with our top-quality vehicle rentals or looking to experience the rich culture and natural wonders of Nepal through our curated tours, Oncall Travel and Tours is dedicated to making your journey unforgettable.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction and service excellence drives us to go beyond expectations, ensuring that every trip with us is not just a journey, but an experience that transcends the ordinary.

Discover the difference with Oncall Travel and Tours and let us make your adventure in Nepal extraordinary!

About Us

What's Our Story?

Based in Chabahil, Kathmandu, World Wide Travel & Tours is a leading name in Nepal's travel industry. With over 22 years of experience, we provide top-notch travel services that cater to a diverse range of travel needs

From an extensive collection of vehicle rentals for those who seek the thrill of independent travel, to our competitively priced airfare solutions, we strive to make every aspect of your journey seamless. Our carefully crafted tour & trekking packages are designed to provide you with an immersive exploration of Nepal's unique wonders.

At World Wide Travel & Tours, we're not just dedicated to meeting your expectations - we aim to exceed them. Our unyielding commitment to customer satisfaction, underscored by our guarantee of service superiority, ensures that every journey you undertake with us transcends the ordinary, entering the realm of the extraordinary.

Come experience the excellence of World Wide Travel & Tours Pvt. Ltd. for yourself!
